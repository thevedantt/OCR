About Us

Our Mission

At EXAGRAM, our mission is to empower students in their exam preparation by providing innovative and efficient solutions that enhance learning and reduce the time spent gathering study materials.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student has access to smart educational tools that facilitate effective and flexible learning, enabling them to achieve their academic goals.

What We Offer

Versatile Question Paper Generator (Testgen): Our advanced question paper generator creates customized question papers based on various patterns, weightage, and past year questions, ensuring that students get a tailored assessment experience.

Exabuddy: This digital assistant helps students interact with both digital and handwritten notes. It uses state-of-the-art technology to retrieve information from PDF documents, making study sessions more productive.

Exaconnect: A community platform that allows students to interact and collaborate, fostering a supportive learning environment where they can share knowledge and resources.

Exavault: Our secure cloud storage solution allows users to store and manage their notes, question papers, and other important study materials, ensuring easy access anytime, anywhere.

Our Team

EXAGRAM is developed by DevDeadlocks, a dedicated team of educators, developers, and innovators who are passionate about transforming the educational experience. We work tirelessly to develop tools that meet the diverse needs of students in today's digital age.
